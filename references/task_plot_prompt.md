# Task Plot Prompt

Create a clean scientific task-flow timeline on a white background with a blank top header band reserved for later overlay. Do not draw any title, subtitle, logo, watermark, people, device, lab room, or decorative scene.

Use three horizontal rows:

1. Low probability offer (12%)
2. Medium probability offer (50%)
3. High probability offer (88%)

For every row, draw the same left-to-right sequence with arrows:

1. Cue
   - Screen: fixation cross.
   - Timing: 1000 ms.
   - Response label: no response.
2. Offer choice
   - Screen: reward probability at top; two option panels.
   - Left option: low effort, low reward, 30 SPACE presses in 7 s, reward 1.00.
   - Right option: high effort, higher reward, 100 SPACE presses in 21 s, variable reward.
   - Timing: 5000 ms.
   - Response label: press F(low) / J(high).
3. Ready
   - Screen: chosen effort requirement and deadline.
   - Timing: 1000 ms.
   - Response label: no response.
4. Effort execution
   - Screen: progress counter for repeated SPACE presses.
   - Timing: 7000 ms low effort or 21000 ms high effort.
   - Response label: press SPACE repeatedly.
5. Effort feedback
   - Screen: complete or failed.
   - Timing: 1000 ms.
   - Response label: no response.
6. Reward feedback
   - Screen: reward win, no win, or no reward if incomplete.
   - Timing: 1000 ms.
   - Response label: no response.
7. ITI
   - Screen: fixation cross.
   - Timing: 1000 ms.
   - Response label: no response.

Keep the chart readable despite seven phases. Use compact boxes, clear arrows, and consistent row spacing.
