# Task Plot Brief

## Task

- Title: EEfRT Task
- Construct: motivation / effort-based decision making
- Paradigm: Effort Expenditure for Rewards Task with probabilistic rewards.

## Rows

- Low probability offer (12%)
- Medium probability offer (50%)
- High probability offer (88%)

## Trial Timeline

1. Cue: 1000 ms fixation, no response.
2. Offer choice: 5000 ms. Show reward probability and low-effort/low-reward vs high-effort/high-reward options. Press F for low effort or J for high effort.
3. Ready: 1000 ms. Show chosen effort requirement and deadline.
4. Effort execution: deadline depends on choice. Low effort requires 30 space presses within 7000 ms; high effort requires 100 space presses within 21000 ms.
5. Effort feedback: 1000 ms. Show completion or failure.
6. Reward feedback: 1000 ms. If effort completed, reward outcome is sampled by probability; otherwise no reward.
7. ITI: 1000 ms fixation, no response.

## Notes

- Probability levels are 12%, 50%, and 88%; hard reward varies across generated offers.
- Choice timeout uses a deterministic fallback choice internally, but the main visual should show the participant choice deadline.
